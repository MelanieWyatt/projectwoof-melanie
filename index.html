Australian Pom

Australian Poms are adorable and fuzzy dogs that require quite a few things. For example, they don't like to be left alone for a long time. They don't adapt well to living in apartments or other small spaces so they need to be in a bigger and more spacious environment. They are very active dogs, so they require daily excersize such as a walk outisde or a lot of play time.

They also require you to take care of the grooming that is associated with them which is going to be day to day care. They require this because they shed a lot and need a regular brushing and shampooing. They do however come with the risk of suffering from teeth/heart issues, kneecap dislocations,and being deaf or blind.
